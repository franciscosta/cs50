Yves is a simple list making app I wrote to help me get things done. I called it Yves after the french painter Yves Klein, a master of art and creativity. It was made as part of my journey to learn to code. More specifically, it was my submission to Problem Set 8 Homepage of Harvard’s CS50x class of 2020.

If you have any thoughts or suggestions, you’re welcome to head over to my personal site and drop me a line.
